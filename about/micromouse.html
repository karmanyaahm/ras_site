Micromouse

Meetings: Sa 8 PM (EER 0.822)

We make the tiny robots in RAS; bite-sized "mice" that race around with the zoomies. We prep for an annual competition down in California where we compete with other university students to explore and traverse a maze the fastest.

Check us out if you want to do:

  • Board level and power/sensor system design
  • High speed control loops
  • Pathfinding and simulation
  • Electronics testing, soldering, and PCB design

Students with previous robotics experience are recommended to join.

For a little more on what we do, check out the Wikipedia page here!
