UT IEEE RAS Fall 2019 General Meeting Dates

This is a list of RAS general meetings for the Fall 2019 semester. General meetings are open to the public. During a general meeting, RAS committees summarize their progress, then a speaker presents on topics of interest, e.g., manufacturing at UT, robotics research, or IoT. Individual committees (say, Micromouse or Demobot) plan additional meeting times through Slack.

Meetings with TBD locations will be updated as we get official confirmation of room scheduling.

RAS leader meetings are held 7-8pm the Sunday before every general meeting.
